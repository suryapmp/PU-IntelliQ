# PU IntelliQ

**The Ultimate Question Paper Generator for Karnataka Pre-University (PU) Education.**

**Author:** Surya Prakash

PU IntelliQ is a high-precision, AI-powered tool designed specifically for faculty and educational institutions in Karnataka. It streamlines the creation of high-quality question paper for **I PU** and **II PU** students, ensuring strict adherence to the **NCERT syllabus** and the **Karnataka PU Board blueprint**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. NCERT-Aligned Chapter & Sub-topic Selection
*   **Class-Specific Content:** Separate, detailed chapter lists for I PU and II PU.
*   **Granular Control:** Select specific sub-topics directly from the NCERT index to target your question paper precisely.
*   **Broad Subject Coverage:** Comprehensive support for Science (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology) and foundational support for Commerce (Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics).

### 2. AI-Powered Question Generation
*   **Gemini 3 Integration:** Leverages the latest AI models to generate high-quality, context-aware questions.
*   **Blueprint Adherence:** Automatically generates a balanced pool of questions across Part A (MCQs, Fill-ups, T/F), Part B, Part C, and Part D.
*   **"Load More" Capability:** Instantly expand your question pool with a single click if you need more variety.

### 3. Automated Institutional Question Bank
*   **Auto-Save Feature:** Every AI-generated question is automatically saved to your local institutional bank.
*   **Persistent Storage:** Your custom bank is stored securely in your browser's local storage, allowing you to build a massive repository of curated questions over time.
*   **Unified View:** Seamlessly mix built-in questions with your AI-generated custom bank.

### 4. Professional Formatting & LaTeX Support
*   **Mathematical Precision:** Full support for complex formulas, scientific notation, and variables using LaTeX (rendered via MathJax).
*   **Board-Standard Layout:** Generates papers with professional headers, clear instructions, and organized sections (Part A to Part D).

### 5. Multi-Format Export
*   **PDF Export:** Generate print-ready PDF documents with a single click.
*   **Word (DOCX) Export:** Export to editable Word documents for further manual refinement.
*   **Answer Key Generation:** Automatically generate a corresponding answer key/marking scheme for every paper.

## 📖 How to Use

1.  **Configure Your Paper:** Select the Class (I/II PU), Subject, and specific Chapter/Sub-topic.
2.  **Choose Generation Mode:**
    *   **Question Bank:** Pull from the existing institutional repository.
    *   **AI Generation:** Let the AI create a fresh pool of questions for you.
3.  **Select Questions:** Browse the generated pool and select the "right choice" of questions for your paper. Your selections are preserved even if you load more questions.
4.  **Preview & Refine:** Review your final paper layout and ensure all sections meet the board's blueprint requirements.
5.  **Export:** Download your professional question paper and answer key in PDF or Word format.
